Crystal Icons 22x22px
---------------------

The icons in this directory are taken from  the KDE version 3.5. They
were designed by Everaldo.com design studio and released unter the
GNU LGPL. They are available here:
http://www.kde-look.org/content/show.php?content=8341
http://www.everaldo.com/

Those icons being prefixed with grale- are modifications for Grale-
specific user actions. They are based on original crystal files.
